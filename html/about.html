about.

I am an industrial designer driven by a fascination with how people interact with objects and the technologies that shape their experiences. Whether it's through physical or digital interfaces, I see design as a tool for pushing boundaries and unlocking new possibilities in human-technology relationships.

My diverse experiences have broadened my understanding of how design can influence behavior and enhance daily life. By applying design thinking and rapid prototyping, I aim to create products that improve accessibility, comfort, and usability for all.

I believe design has the power to transform lives, and I am dedicated to crafting thoughtful, innovative solutions that meet the complex needs of individuals and communities. Through every project, I strive to make technology more intuitive, accessible, and life-enhancing.
